4. Underconstrained Variables

These items prevent upgrading "PROVISIONAL" entries to "PASS":

  • Annual-by-annual fitted \((r_y, \delta_y)\) at Óbidos and Manaus from long daily/monthly series. Needed to compute regime occupancy \(P(\text{two-maxima})\). Dependency: F-1, CL-3 upgrade gate.
  • Explicit rainfall harmonic fits for defined North-lump / South-lump masks from a chosen gridded rainfall product (GPCC/TRMM/GPM), yielding \(A_{ik}, \phi_{ik}\) and their interannual drift.
  • Quantified kernel drift \(G_i(\tau, t)\) proxies — land-use change, floodplain roughness, channel morphology — and/or parametric \(\tau(Q)\) relationships. Dependency: F-3.
  • Stage-to-discharge rating curves to translate level thresholds into \(Q_\mathrm{fp}\) if hysteresis modelling is pursued.
